Delete Account

Today, Aligned Account Deletion

`Today, Aligned` allows users to delete their account directly in the app. Once deletion is completed, core account-linked app data is deleted immediately and the user is signed out.

Effective date: March 27, 2026 Operator: studio nani Contact: studionani.official@google.com
한국어 English 日本語

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Sign in to the app.
  2. Open the `My Saju` screen.
  3. Select `Delete account`.
  4. Confirm the action to complete deletion.

What is deleted immediately

  • The account
  • Saved profile information
  • Recent lookup history
  • Lookup usage records
  • Current entitlement status

Additional notes

  • The app signs the user out automatically after deletion is completed.
  • Deleting the app alone may not automatically delete remote account data.
  • Some purchase-related records may be retained separately under legal or operational requirements for purchase verification, dispute handling, or operational audit purposes.
  • The app does not currently provide a self-service feature to delete only selected portions of data without deleting the account.
