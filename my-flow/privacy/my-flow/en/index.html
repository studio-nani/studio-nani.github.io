Today, Aligned Privacy Policy

`Today, Aligned` is a Saju-based personal report app. We process personal data only to the extent needed to save profiles, support sign-in, restore purchases, and show recent lookups. This policy applies only to the `Today, Aligned` app.

Effective date: March 27, 2026 Operator: studio nani Contact: support@studionani.io

1. Information We Collect

Category Examples Purpose
Account information Email address, sign-in provider identifiers, user ID Sign-in, account identification, purchase restore, separating data by user
Profile information Profile name, birth date, birth time, solar/lunar/leap month status Create a personal Saju profile and generate reports

2. How We Use Information

  • To save user profiles and generate personal reports
  • To support sign-in, account identification, and cross-device restore
  • To reflect purchase history and entitlement status
  • To show recent lookups and basic usage records
  • To monitor service stability and respond to issues

3. Third-Party Services

`Today, Aligned` uses the following external services:

  • Supabase: authentication, user profile storage, entitlement and purchase sync
  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In: social sign-in
  • Google Play / Apple App Store: in-app purchases and restore processing

The actual processing scope of each service is also subject to that provider's own policies.

4. Retention and Deletion

  • Data tied to a signed-in account may be retained while the account remains active.
  • Local device data may be removed when the app is deleted or local storage is reset.
  • When account deletion is requested, related data will be deleted or de-identified except where retention is required for operation or law.

5. Your Rights

  • Users may contact us by email regarding sign-in information, profile data, and purchase-related matters.
  • Requests related to account deletion or data deletion may be submitted to the contact below.
  • Deleting the app alone may not automatically delete remote account data.

6. Children's Privacy

`Today, Aligned` is not designed as an app primarily directed to children, and it is not operated with children as its primary target audience.
